# Taste Guidelines

**Type:** STABLE
**Created:** 2026-02-03
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-03

---

## Good Examples

- **Landing page (app/page.tsx)** - Reference for overall aesthetic
- Neat, compact layouts
- Pixel icons (pixelarticons)
- Glass effects where appropriate

## Anti-Patterns (Never Do)

- Generic SaaS look (rounded corners, gradients, drop shadows)
- Sparse/empty feeling (too much whitespace)
- Over-animated loading states
- Shortcuts or hacks - always elegant solutions

## Design Defaults

| Attribute | Value | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Corners | Sharp only (0-2px max) | No rounding |
| Motion | Snappy (100-200ms) | Purpose over decoration |
| Density | Compact, information-rich | Avoid sparse layouts |
| Approach | Simple and elegant | Not overengineered |

## Autonomy Guidelines

- **Full autonomy for bugs** - Fix critical/high bugs without asking
- **Sign-off required for:** Architecture decisions only
- **Default approach:** Simple and elegant, no shortcuts or hacks
